The landlord reserves the right to enter the property for inspections, repairs, or emergencies with at least 24-hour notice to the tenant, except in the case of an emergency.

Either party may terminate the lease with a 30-day written notice. In the case of early termination by the tenant, the security deposit will be forfeited.
